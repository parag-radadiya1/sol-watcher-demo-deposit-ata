## ✨ Meaning / Symbolism
Yellow Topaz and Citrine represent clarity, abundance, positivity, and emotional balance. They are widely associated with uplifting energy and renewed confidence.

---

## 🌟 Key Benefits
- Enhances confidence and personal power  
- Attracts prosperity and abundance  
- Improves mood and emotional balance  
- Supports clarity in decision‑making  
- Encourages creativity and optimism  

---

## 🔮 Planetary Association
- **Topaz / Yellow Topaz** → Jupiter  
- **Citrine** → Sun / Jupiter  

---

## 🔥 Chakra Connection
- **Solar Plexus Chakra** – boosts confidence, motivation, and personal energy.

---

## 🧭 How to Wear
- **Day:** Thursday  
- **Metal:** Gold  
- **Finger:** Index finger  
- **Recommended Weight:** 5–7 carats  

---

## 🧼 Cleansing & Charging
- Rinse under clean water  
- Keep in early morning sunlight for 10–15 minutes  
- Optional: Cleanse with incense or sound  

---

## 🔁 Substitute Stone
- **Citrine** can be used as a substitute for Yellow Topaz.
